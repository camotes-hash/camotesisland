About Camotes

Known as the "Lost Horizon of the North", Camotes Island is a hidden paradise in Cebu, Philippines. The island is composed of four municipalities: Poro, Tudela, San Francisco, and Pilar. With pristine beaches, caves, lakes, and rich culture, Camotes offers the perfect escape for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation.
