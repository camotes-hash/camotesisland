Welcome to Camotes

Known as the "Lost Horizon of the South", Camotes Island is a tropical paradise in Cebu, Philippines. With white sandy beaches, hidden caves, waterfalls, and rich culture, Camotes is the perfect escape for adventurers and nature lovers.

Top Attractions

Santiago Bay

A stunning white sand beach with crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming and relaxing.

Timubo Cave

Explore this enchanting cave with a natural underground pool.

Lake Danao

The largest lake in Cebu, perfect for kayaking and nature sightseeing.
